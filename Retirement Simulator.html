Retirement Simulator

This is a simulation of your net worth over time. It is based on your current age, income, expenses, and other factors. It is not a guarantee of your future net worth, but it is a good starting point.

Assumptions:

  • Market returns are based on historical S&P 500 returns
  • Inflation is based on historical CPI
  • Taxes are based on current laws (only federal income tax is considered)
  • Expenses are split between housing, medical, and other expenses
  • Kids' ages are used to calculate potential private school expenses
  • Expenses are reduced with age if needed (2% per year starting at age 60 by upto 25%)
